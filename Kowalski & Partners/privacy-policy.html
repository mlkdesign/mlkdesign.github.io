Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Kowalski & Partners collects, uses, and protects personal information submitted through our website and client intake forms.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, company details, and any information you voluntarily provide when contacting us or requesting legal assistance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to respond to your inquiry, manage our client relationship, prepare internal case notes, and communicate about our services. We may also use this information to improve our website and client experience.

3. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. Access is limited to authorized team members who need the information for legitimate business purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with service providers who support our operations, such as secure email or hosting providers, where necessary and under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable legal and professional obligations.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at office@kowalski.pl.
